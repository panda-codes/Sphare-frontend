Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are some common questions and answers to help you navigate our services at SphareHealth.

You can easily book an appointment by visiting our appointment page. Simply fill out the form with your details, select your preferred department, date, and time, and submit the request. You will receive a confirmation email shortly.
SphareHealth offers a wide range of medical services, including general consultations, specialized treatments, emergency care, radiology and imaging, surgery, and more. For a complete list of our services, please visit our Services page.
Yes, you can choose your preferred doctor based on availability. When booking an appointment, select the doctor of your choice from the dropdown menu. If the doctor is unavailable, our system will suggest alternative doctors.
Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card, any medical records related to your condition, and a list of any medications you are currently taking. If you have any previous test results, such as blood tests or imaging reports, please bring them as well.
Our office hours are from Monday to Friday, 8:30 AM to 6:00 PM. We also offer emergency services 24/7. For specific department hours, please contact us directly or visit the department's page on our website.

If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please contact us at least 24 hours in advance. You can call our office or manage your appointment through the confirmation email you received.
